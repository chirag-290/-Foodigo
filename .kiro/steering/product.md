# Product Overview

A full-stack food ordering web application for takeaway and online delivery (similar to Zomato). Users can browse restaurants, filter by cuisine/rating/price, add items to cart, and place orders with Cash on Delivery (COD) payment.

Key features:
- User authentication with JWT
- Restaurant browsing with filters and sorting
- Menu management with veg/non-veg options
- Shopping cart functionality
- Order placement and tracking
- Multiple delivery address management
